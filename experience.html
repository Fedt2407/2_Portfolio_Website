Ars longa, vita brevis

What I have done
so far

I have a background in business economics and a Master's in Business Administration. I gained my experience first as a project manager in the aerospace sector and later in the areas of planning and management of strategic projects in the retail and fashion sectors, becoming responsible for strategic planning at the Autogrill Group from 2006 to 2008 and at the DIESEL Group from 2009 to 2017.

During my time in the company, I coordinated various teams and managed projects in organization, CRM, category management, retail, technological transition and improvement of production processes.

In 2017, I decided to leave the employee life to dedicate myself to entrepreneurial projects.

In 2018, I founded WOOLF Intelligent Drive, a company that deals with the design and distribution of innovative products for driving support, winner of numerous awards including the Seal of Excellence under the European Union's Horizon 2020 program and the CES Innovation Award in Las Vegas for the best wearable technologies worldwide.

I am passionate about digital technologies and in recent years I have extended my skills in the areas of Web and App development.

My experience in managing numerous complex projects in diversified multinational contexts allows me to clearly understand the main industrial sectors and to support medium-sized companies in defining the strategy and in the planning and execution of special and highly transformative projects.
